About the Rabbitry

Hello, and Welcome to one of the largest rabbitries in LL’s community which is a SIM which is a SIM rabbit breeding game. We were originally established under the name Haley Nicholle’s Rabbitry in 1/03/2017. As a very small rabbitry learning how to play the game since we were introduced by a friend, once we first started we breed under the breeding prefix HNR when we were able to register our first 10 litters to get a breeding prefix then we changed our name for the second time to Haley Nicholle’s Lops in 2017 once again due to us breeding English Lops as our main breed which we had changed our breeding prefix to HNLOP. In the beginning of the new year of 2018 we were fortunate to buy credits to expand our rabbitry the way you see it as today, during this time we also helped others expand as well. After all of the name changes we finally needed to settle for a new rabbitry name that fit us, our final name change was made on 5/12/2018. to what it is now Dairyland Bunnies Inc. this name was selected from a contest that I held for a new rabbitry name and also breeding prefix which we now use CATTL.

As you see the rabbitry that we have today, we could not have so successful or even be possible without the wonderful different breeders within LL that has helped us, we have switched our original breed which we did over a year which was English Lops, which we still have some that linger within our hutches for show purposes, but since then we have transitioned our main focus and which is our main breed to Holland Lops which now our rabbitry is filled with them.
