About Us

Rabbitry Hours

I have just came back from a break due to school, but since I am on break for the winter we have had the time to be active once again. Since my rabbitry does have a lot of rabbits we are on a lot many times during the day checking in on our rabbitry. The daily routine to run my rabbitry consists of : looking for any missed messages from the night before, treating our animals of any illnesses or routine vet care,vet sexing new litters that might have arrived after rollover, caring for my animals, participating in shows this includes group shows and agility with all of my animals, leveling up animals after shows,setting up breedings between my animals, and updating stats when I can.

My goal for the new year is to be active daily when I go back to school so I can continue my projects and also see where my rabbitry takes me for the new year.
